    Each PC is built on an individual workstation ( No Production Line Here !! ) by one technician from beginning to end . Every stage is overseen by the same technician who is both an enthusiast and a qualified technical services engineer. Our technicians are not only highly skilled at their jobs they are also enthusiasts which means our systems are built by enthusiasts for enthusiasts . We do everything in our power to avoid any issues when you receive your New Custom PC and therefore all PCs must pass this multi point testing procedure.
